About 'X'

What was formerly known as 'Twitter' was the vision of founder Elon Musk . Musk imagined a space where people can freely share their thoughts, experiences, lives, and moments in real time. In July 2023, Musk established X Holdings, an organization that absorbed 'Twitter' and turned it into X.

It's no secret that 'Twitter' started to get used for the wrong reasons.

Friends BASHING friends.

People ATTACKING others.

By rebranding to X, we hope that users look at our space in a different way. We hope that people are able to share and express their free speech in a way that doesn't tarnish our name, or their own.

X. It's What's Happening.
